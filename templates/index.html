Habit Formula Context

The projection model weights performance parameters. Specifically:

  • Study Hours and Distraction Hours are combined to evaluate effective study time.
  • Sleep Hours are critical to cognitive functions and act as a score multiplier in training.
  • Previous score acts as the student's foundation level.
